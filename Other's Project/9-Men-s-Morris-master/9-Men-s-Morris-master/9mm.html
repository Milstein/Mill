9 Men's Morris

Rules:
1. In the initial stage, players take turns placing 11 pieces each.
2. After all pieces have been placed, players move by sliding pieces to open, adjacent positions.
3. A player completes a "mill" by positioning three pieces in a line.
4. When a player completes a mill, she removes one of her opponent's pieces.
5. However, any piece that forms part of a mill cannot be removed.
6. The game ends when one player is left with no legal plays.
